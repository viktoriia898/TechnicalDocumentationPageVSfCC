Who we are
Abous us

VS HOTEL is an exciting place for you and your family to enjoy your vacation and live out unforgettable experiences. Our hotels are focused on bringing the best possible trip for our customers. We care about people and the environment, so as we move forward with enhancing the quality of our services, we also make sure to keep making our hotels eco-friendly, so everybody can enjoy their time off while caring for the planet.

Our Environmental Goals:

  • Become Carbon Neutral by year 2030;
  • Become Zero-Waste by year 2035;
  • Use 100% renewable energy by year 2030;
Services
All for our guests!

Making our guests comfortable is our #1 Priority, so we have made accomodations to suit our guests no matter their need. We continue to improve our hotels with the services we provide, so that you can enjoy the best of what the world has to offer with the outmost comfort.

What we offer:

  • Spacious rooms for families: rooms with enough space for 3+ people to spend their time in;
  • Eco-friendly stay: equipt with compost drop-offs, solar panels, recycling bins, and more. Enjoy your stay while taking care of the planet;
  • Food for all: our menus are built to suit everyone's needs (vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, children's menu, and more!)
  • Accesability: the hotel and every room can be accessed with a wheelchair and a stroller, and our staff is ready to provide you with any other necessities.
  • Different meetings and events each week to suit your interests!
